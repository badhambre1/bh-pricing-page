Privacy Policy

BH Pricing — Last updated: February 10, 2026

What data does this app access?

BH Pricing reads a single cart attribute ("Checkout-Method") at checkout to determine whether an order qualifies for a local discount. It also reads app-owned configuration settings stored in a Shopify metafield.

What data does this app store?

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It has no external server or database. All logic runs within Shopify's infrastructure as a Shopify Function.

Third-party services

This app does not share data with any third-party services.
